Immigration

Visa Services

We prepare and lodge applications across all major visa streams. Every matter is handled personally by Dimitri Bourne.

Subclass 820 / 801

Partner Visa

For spouses and de facto partners of Australian citizens or permanent residents. Temporary to permanent two-stage pathway. Your marriage must be legal under Australian Law.

Subclass 189 / 190

Skilled & Nominated

Points-tested visa for skilled workers. The 190 requires state or territory nomination — we manage the full EOI process, skills assessment and lodgement.

Subclass 482

Temporary Skill Shortage

Employer-sponsored work visa allowing skilled overseas workers to fill genuine vacancies. Standard Business Sponsorship with short and medium-term streams.

Subclass 500

Student Visa

This visa allows you to stay in Australia to study full-time in a recognised educational institution. You can study anything from certificate level up to a doctorate.

Subclass 600

Visitor Visa

Short-stay tourism, family visit or business visitor visa. We advise on the appropriate stream and prepare a compelling application package.

Subclass 192

Pacific Engagement

For nationals of Pacific Island countries and Timor-Leste, including PNG. Ballot-based permanent residence pathway. We manage ballot and full application.

Subclass 186

Employer Nomination

Permanent residence through employer nomination. Direct entry, temporary residence transition and labour agreement streams. Full-time position required.

Subclass 485

Graduate Visa

The Temporary Skilled Graduate visa offers international graduates with an Australian education a way to gain valuable work experience in Australia for 18 to 48 months.
